Who Are We?

Sahl is a mobile bill payment app in Egypt, offering users the convenience of paying bills for over 50 services including electricity, gas, water, and mobile. We use NFC technology to allow users to recharge prepaid cards anytime, anywhere. Additionally, we've launched B2B products to expand our audience by providing utility services and payment acceptance to other companies.
